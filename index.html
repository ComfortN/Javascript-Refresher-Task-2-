Memory Game

How to Play

  • Click on any two cards to flip them over and reveal the letters on their back sides.
  • If the letters on the two flipped cards match, those cards will remain face up, indicating they have been successfully matched.
  • If the letters do not match, the cards will flip back over to their face-down position after a short delay.
  • Continue flipping pairs of cards until all matching pairs have been found.
